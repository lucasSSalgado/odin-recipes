Farofa

Image

INGREDIENTS

  • 50 g bacon cut into cubes or strips
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 3 crushed garlic cloves
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 and 1/2 cup of cassava flour
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1 grated carrot
  • olive oil, salt and red pepper (tabasco) to taste
  • parsley and chives to taste

HOW TO DO

  • Fry the bacon in a drizzle of oil until it is very crispy.
  • Put the onion and let it cook for 2 minutes. Add the garlic and mix well.
  • Throw the eggs over, without stirring. When they are well cooked, mix carefully, to leave large pieces.
  • Put the flour and the necessary seasonings (salt and pepper) and let it fry for about 4 minutes, always stirring.
  • Lastly, put carrots, tomatoes, parsley and onions. Leave 2 minutes in the fire and turn it off.
